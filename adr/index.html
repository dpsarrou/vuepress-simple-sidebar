# Overview

Architecture Decision Records is an essential documentation for any software project because they capture the most important architectural decisions, their context and their consequences. They can be used to understand why some choices where made, what are the consequences of those choices and check whether the forces that forced such a choice are still valid to date. If not, maybe its time for an updated decision that can simplify the codebase.
